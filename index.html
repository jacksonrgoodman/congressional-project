Senator Marsha Blackburn

Website Twitter

Senator Blackburn is the senior senator from Tennessee and is a Republican. She has served since Jan 3, 2019. Blackburn is next up for reelection in 2024 and serves until Jan 3, 2025.

Committees

Senator Marsha Blackburn is a member of these Committees:

  1. Senate Committee on Armed Services
  2. Senate Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation
  3. Senate Committee on the Judiciary
  4. Senate Committee on Veterans' Affairs

Legislation

Senator Marsha Blackburn has sponsored over 214 pieces of legislation including:

  • S. 298: A bill to require the Government Accountability Office to study the role pharmaceutical benefit managers play in the pharmaceutical supply chain and provide Congress with appropriate policy recommendations, and for other purposes.
  • S. 88: A bill to amend title X of the Public Health Service Act to prohibit family planning grants from being awarded to any entity that performs abortions, and for other purposes.
  • S. 113: A bill to require providers of broadband internet access service and edge services to clearly and conspicuously notify users of the privacy policies of those providers, to give users opt-in or opt-out approval rights with respect to the use of, disclosure of, and access to user information collected by those providers based on the level of sensitivity of the information, and for other purposes.
